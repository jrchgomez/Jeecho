Bakkayan Beach Resort: Your Gateway to Paradise

Nestled on the stunning shores of BanayBanay, Davao Oriental, Bakkayan Beach Resort has been welcoming guests. Our story began with a vision to create a haven where travelers could immerse themselves in the beauty of the Philippines, experience the warmth of Filipino hospitality, and create lasting memories.

Over the years, we've grown into a beloved destination, renowned for our pristine beachfront location, comfortable accommodations, and dedication to exceptional service.
